About Me

A little bit of everything, on purpose.

I'm Avishek — part explorer, developer, singer-songwriter. I don't see these as separate lanes; they all come from the same habit of paying attention, then making something out of what I notice.

Whether it's a line of code, a new place, or a half-finished song, I like the process of turning curiosity into something real. This site is where all of that lives in one place.
